About us
HAIR VIETNAM FACTROY
Hairvietnam is one of the most reliable hair manufacturers in Vietnam and Southeast Asia. The company was founded in early 1990, two decades ago. In the beginning, we were only a small factory, we produced hair extensions products for many ecommercial companies to export worldwide. As time goes by, the company has grown rapidly in the hair industry. And finally in 2008, we were able to sell hair separately and has been having customers comes from all over the world.
